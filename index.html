Micro Farm

MicroFARM is a cost-effective option for monitoring crops, disease risk, and pinpointing spray windows. MicroFARM can be used on its own or combined with other METOS Canada equipment.

Field Essentials

FieldESSENTIALS is cost effective for deploying many units over a wide area where NB-IoT or a LoRa gateway is available. FieldESSENTIALS can be used on its own or combined with other METOS Canada packages, sensors, and equipment.

Weather Pro

WeatherPRO is the ultimate IoT station package for sophisticated field monitoring and forecasting. Highly robust and expandable, WeatherPRO provides near real-time, weather-related site conditions. WeatherPRO can be used on its own or combined with other METOS Canada equipment.
